About Biggboss 5 season (Telugu)

Introduction

Bigg Boss 5 is the fifth season of the Telugu version of the Indian reality television series Bigg Boss based on Dutch series Big Brother. The show premiered on 5 September 2021 on Star Maa and Disney+ Hotstar with Akkineni Nagarjuna as a host for the third time


Delay

The fifth season of the show was planned to launch in June 2021, but it was delayed due to the second wave of the COVID-19 pandemic in India.[2]


Eye Logo

The eye of Bigg Boss is shaped in the form of a maze. It is a hint at how living in the house is a maze and only a few people can come out of it unaffected.


Teaser

The show organizers officially released the eye logo teaser on 1 August 2021. The promo shoots of the show were directed by Prasanth Varma.[3][4] On 26 August 2021, it is confirmed that the show will premiere on 5 September 2021 on Star Maa.[5]


Bigg Boss 5 Buzzz

Bigg Boss 5 Buzzz is an Indian Telugu-language television talk show about the reality television series Bigg Boss Telugu. The host Ariyana Glory conducts the talk show with the evicted contestants of Bigg Boss 5 on every Monday. The show premiered on 13 September 2021 onwards on Star Maa Music. The unseen portions of episodes that were not aired on television are streamed on Disney+ Hotstar and aired on Star Maa Music as Bigg Boss 5 Buzzz
